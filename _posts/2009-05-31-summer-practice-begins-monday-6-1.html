Summer Team Practice begins this coming Monday, June 1st. You can still register by fax or by filling out the registration form Summer Swim Team</a> and bringing it with you to the clinic on Monday. It looks like the weather may cooperate until later in the afternoon/evening.

If you ordered on line you can pick up your order. KEIFER will now be at our practice this Monday the 8th of June from 4:45 to 6:30 PM in the concession area. If the weather is too bad they will be there Tuesday at the same time, You may order additional "Lightning" apparel as well as goggles, practice suits, and many other supplies. Lightning apparel may be previewed and also ordered at "Lightning Gear"</a> on this site

Those new to the team: Please enter the pool through the "All Purpose Room", check in with a coach and then go to the concession area. Parents are also permitted in the concession area before and during practice,  but not on pool deck during the practice.
